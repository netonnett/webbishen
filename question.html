Product information


What varieties of potatoes do you offer on your website?

We offer a big variety of potatoes on our website.
Our most popular products are the classic potato which we offer in different sizes from big to small…
We also offer the classic potato in two different shapes, oval and round for a more customised experience.
Other than that you can find sweet potatoes from big to small sizes as well as oval and round shaped sweet potatoes.
Something unique with our potatoes is that the ethnicity of the potatoes are up to you. You can choose to buy potatoes from over 190+ countries and we guarantee the same freshness.


Are certain potato varieties only available during specific seasons?

While we try to always offer all types of potatoes under a certain season that can be challenging.
Throughout the year our potato varieties are always available.
However if something would come up and the availability of a potato would change we inform our customers as soon as possible.
We also try to keep our website updated everyday to inform about product quantity and other important information.

Ordering and delivery


What is the minimum and maximum quantity of potatoes that customers can order?

The maximum amount of potatoes you can order on our website is 50 kilograms.
The minimum amount is one potato.
However if you wish for an amount of potatoes that exceed the maximum and/or the minimum amount then we recommend contacting us.
Usually for special requests we ship more or less quantities of potatoes than available on our website.


What regions or countries do you ship potatoes to?

We ship our potatoes worldwide anywhere.
No matter where you are you will have access to the full Spudly page and will be able to order everything we offer.

Quality Assurance


Do you have any quality guarantee or return policy?

We always guarantee 100% satisfaction amongst our customers. Since you can customise the potato it's guaranteed that you will get exactly what you wished for.
While we always try to guarantee 100% customer satisfaction it's hard to always give the exact same size or shape everytime.
However we do guarantee that the potatoes are not rotten or have any type of unwanted spots.
You can also return our products for up to one week after receiving the items. Just contact us at our "contact us" page and write your complaint and we will send you a refund.

Pricing


Are there any discounts or promotions available for bulk purchases?

Sometimes we offer special discounts around holidays and other types of events.
This is mainly because we know that potatoes are a crucial part of the holiday table.
For bigger bulk purchases we do offer a small discount.
If it's a company or school buying our potatoes we give a promotion mainly because of the amount of potatoes.
We also offer to do a contract with companies that wish to buy our products regularly. In those contracts the price is determined by the amount of potatoes per month and negotiation about the price.

Can customers view the prices of potatoes per unit or weight on the website?

Since the minimum amount of potatoes on our website is one potato the price varies depending on unit and weight.
If you buy one potato the price will depend on the unit. However, there are options to buy 0.5kg all the way to 50kg. Then the price will be determined by the weight.

Costumer service


What are your customer service hours?

You will always be able to send us an email no matter the time. Our team is working hard everyday to be able to answer your questions as fast as possible. To get a reply to an email usually takes around 1-2 working days.
However, the fastest way to reach us is by phone. Our phone number you can find at our contact us page. The telephone customer service is open from 9 am to 6 pm monday-friday.
On the weekends our hours are unfortunately shorter due to lack of workers. On the weekend you can reach us from 13 pm to 16 pm.
If you have any further questions we recommend checking this page out, frequently asked questions, before contacting us.

How do you use customer feedback to improve your services?

We take customer feedback at full seriousness, after all it's our customers that use the website the most.
We always appreciate the feedback, no matter the type. Your feedback is what is making Spudly a better website and company everyday.
When we get a complaint or any type of feedback we look into the problem and brainstorm ideas to immediately improve the problem and make it better.
Sometimes it's hard to find the flaws in our own website. That is why we appreciate the feedback from our customers so we can improve Spudly and make it a special place.
Don't hesitate to write your own feedback to us at our contact us page. We would love to hear your thoughts and ideas about Spudly, since we together are what makes Spudly so magical and real…

Recipes


Do you provide any recipes or cooking tips on your website for the potatoes you sell?

As much as we love cooking with potatoes, Spudly does not offer recipes of their own. However we do recommend delish at our about our potatoes page.
There you can find 85 potato recipes that are fun and easy to make.
One day however, we wish to be able to offer our own potato recipes but currently that is not possible.
As for now, we recommend checking out delish, bbc good food, the pioneer woman and taste of home, for a variety of recipes that you can make from your potatoes.
